Who We Are

Medlink Clinic is a 100% black owned and managed Human Consulting Company based in Pretoria, South Africa. Our business activities are focused on Human Resources Consulting, Medico Legal services, Psychometric Assessments, Career Guidance and are not restricted in nature. We are a young company looking to push the boundaries of excellence and committed to delivering exceptional service to its clients. Our clients experience special treatment from our team due to our company being small medium enterprise.




Improve Quality

We ensure all our experts have undertaken accredited Medicolegal Training, with all new experts undertaking the Bond Solon Medical Expect Training Courses.

Ease Communication

It can be challenging for Solicitors to communicate with Medical Experts. Think Medicolegal provides a single point of contact between both.

Reduce Cost

As government legislation tightens and linkage increases, there is an overall need to reduce litigation costs.We remove traditional intermediary fees.

Reduce Complexity
